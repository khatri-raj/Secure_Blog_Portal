Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

To create an account, click the "Register" link in the navigation or visit the signup page. Fill in your username, email, and password, then submit the form. You’ll be logged in automatically and can start blogging.

Our Blog Portal uses role-based access control (RBAC):
  • Admin: Manages all blogs and users.
  • Editor: Reviews and publishes blogs.
  • Author: Creates and edits their own blogs.
  • Reader: Views published blogs.
Contact an admin to change your role.

If you’re an Author or higher, go to the Add Blog page to create a post. To edit, visit the All Blogs page, find your post, and click "Edit." Only Admins and Editors can edit others’ posts.

On the Login page, click "Forgot password?" to reset your password. Enter your email address, and we’ll send a link to create a new password.

For further assistance, email our support team at support@blogportal.com. We’ll respond within 24 hours.
